## India Space Lab — CanSat Program *(Ex-Trainee)*

> *"Designing for space means designing for failure — and ensuring it never happens."*

As part of India Space Lab's competitive **CanSat Program**, I engineered a **dual-redundant aerospace-grade PCB** capable of surviving the mechanical and thermal stresses of simulated satellite deployment.

- Designed full schematic, PCB routing, and Gerber files in **EasyEDA**
- Implemented redundancy architecture for mission-critical sensor reliability
- Validated design against aerospace PCB fabrication standards
- Part of a rigorous training pipeline modelled on real satellite engineering workflows

**Stack:** EasyEDA · Embedded Systems · PCB Design & Fabrication · Aerospace Engineering Principles
